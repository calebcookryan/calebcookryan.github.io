## Hi! I'm Caleb Ryan

I am a MSc student in the Department of Biology at the University of Waterloo in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. My current research is focused on the role that individuality plays in creating life-history differences between members of the same population. I am primarily interested in how personality differences between female little brown bats ( _Myotis lucifugus_) contributes to the social organization of summer maternity colonies. 

While bat focused, my past (and hopefully future!) research interests are broad
